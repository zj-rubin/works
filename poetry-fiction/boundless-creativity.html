Boundless Creativity
Published: February 12, 2025 | 14th of Sh’vat, 5785 | Category: Poetry, Tanka, EN, JP
Restless unbounded
Forms of sharp zither music
Poets and swordsmiths
Dance with unmapped flames around
Moonlit Shinnyo-dō temple
Avowed secret truths
Ubiquitous seclusion
A single taste in
Soft bouncing zither songs at
Moonlit Shinnyo-dō temple
Japanese Translation
Translated by a friend
限界が無いの
清脆琴音楽の像
詩人と刀匠
未図火と踊る
月明かり真如堂
公然した神秘
普遍的隔離
唯一の味は
安神琴の歌
月明かり真如堂
